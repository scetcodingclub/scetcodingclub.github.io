SparkQuest25

Welcome to SparkQuest, a 3-day idea hackathon where creativity
meets innovation! This is the perfect platform to pitch real-life problems and solutions that can be built into reality. SparkQuest challenges you to dream big, share your bold ideas, and take the first steps toward turning them into something tangible.

The goal of SparkQuest is to provide a platform where participants can take their raw ideas, refine them with the help of mentors,and then present them to a panel of judges for feedback and recognition

Whether you're passionate about tech, social innovation, or just have a brilliant concept waiting to be brought to life—this is your moment.

Participants not only compete for prizes but also gain valuable experience, mentorship, and networking opportunities that can help turn their ideas into reality.
